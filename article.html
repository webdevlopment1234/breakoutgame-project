Game Overview

The Breakout Ball Game is a classic arcade game where the player controls a paddle to bounce a ball and break bricks. The objective is to clear all the bricks without letting the ball fall below the paddle.

How to Play

  1. Objective: Keep the ball in play and break all the bricks.
  2. Controls:
    • Move Paddle Left: Press the Left Arrow Key.
    • Move Paddle Right: Press the Right Arrow Key.
  3. Gameplay: Use the paddle to hit the ball back up. If the ball falls below the paddle, you lose a life.
  4. Winning the Game: Clear all bricks to win!

Game Features

  • Multiple Levels with Increasing Difficulty
  • Power-ups to Enhance Gameplay
  • Score Tracking and Leaderboards
