Breakout Ball Game - Medium Level

Challenges and Strategies for the Medium Level

Medium Level Overview

The Medium Level of the Breakout Ball Game offers a balanced challenge, perfect for players looking to improve their skills without overwhelming difficulty.

Gameplay Adjustments

  • Time Limit: 2 minutes to clear the level.
  • Lives: Start with 2 lives.
  • Brick Toughness: Bricks require more hits to break compared to the Easy Level.

Strategies for Success

  • Focus on hitting the bricks in clusters to maximize your score.
  • Keep an eye on the ball's trajectory to anticipate its movement.
  • Utilize power-ups wisely to gain an advantage.
